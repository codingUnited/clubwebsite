Welcome to Coding United

A student-led tech club dedicated to building real-world projects, supporting academic success, and growing together through hands-on coding, curiosity, and collaboration. We are not here to fit in. We are here to build, to learn, and to become.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to foster a vibrant and inclusive community of student developers, empowering everyone with the skills and experience to succeed in the world of technology.

Club Projects

Group projects are a key part of building a strong coding portfolio. The club maintains 2-3 active projects at all times, allowing members to participate and contribute as their schedule permits. This provides valuable experience working with other people's codebases and collaborating as a team to solve real-world problems, skills every new developer needs.

Open Office Hours

Being an online student can feel isolating, you may think you're on your own with no one to turn to for advice. That's why we started our Open Office Hours initiative. Members can drop in to ask questions, get debugging help, or just do silent co-working. Sometimes, just knowing you're not alone on this journey makes all the difference.

Coding Challenges

We host regular, language-agnostic coding challenges to help members grow their skills. These challenges push you out of your comfort zone and encourage problem-solving from different angles and all with the goal of shaping more well-rounded developers.

Coding Education and Guidance

We curate as many free educational resources as possible for club members, no matter what path they're on. Whether it's game development, AI, or quantum computing, we aim to make learning accessible. Sometimes the biggest barrier to success is simply not knowing what's available to you.

Book Club

No matter your career goals, reading is essential. In tech's ever-changing landscape, staying informed is even more important. Our Book Club holds polls on what topics to explore and meets biweekly to discuss assigned chapters. It helps us stay accountable and grow beyond the standard curriculum.

Fostering Leadership

We provide opportunities for members to have their voices heard and take on leadership roles. Whether it's leading a club project, assisting with administrative tasks, or running meetings, we encourage members to step up and build their communication and leadership skills.
